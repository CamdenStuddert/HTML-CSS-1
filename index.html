The Town of Leeds

Leeds is in Washington County Utah. It is a great location
due to it being in the middle of many state and national parks.
The town has been around for while. The historical sites include
the silver reef mines and 15 houses built in the 19th
century on Main Street.

Places to Visit

  • Silver Reef Museum
  • Hike he Red Cliffs Trail (Be sure to bring a swimsuit in the summer!)
  • Visit the Dinosaur Footprints
  • Make it to the Yant Flat
  • Camp in the Red Cliffs Recreation Area
